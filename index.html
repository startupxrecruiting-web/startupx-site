The process

Simple. Fast. No surprises.

01
Week 1

We align before we source.

We run a focused discovery conversation to understand the role deeply. We want to know what the role is, what success looks like and why this hire matters right now. No intake form. Just direct alignment before we start sourcing.

Discovery call → written brief → search begins
02
Week 1–2

We match. You review.

We pull from our actively managed pipeline of pre-vetted, startup-ready operators. People we've already identified and assessed before your search begins. You get 2–3 vetted profiles with a write-up on why each one is worth your time.

2–3 vetted candidates → in 10 days or less
03
Ongoing

We present our recommendation. You make the final call.

We don't just deliver names. We give you a ranked recommendation and our informed opinion on fit. We coordinate interviews, manage communication, and stay involved through close. You make the decision; we handle everything around it.

$0 until day one → no hidden fees
