What this network is

Not a job board.
A curated talent pipeline.

StartupX is building a curated network of candidates interested in working with early-stage startups. We work directly with founders and revenue leaders at Seed to Series A companies to fill critical revenue and GTM roles.

When you submit your profile, you're not applying to a specific job. We keep you in mind, and when there's a strong match — on role type, stage, and fit — we reach out with the details.

We do not blast your resume everywhere. We do not run you through a generic screening call. We look for relevant fit and make targeted introductions.

If you're interested in startup revenue, GTM, RevOps, partnerships, customer-facing, or operator roles — and you want to work at an early-stage company — this is where you should be.
